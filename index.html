About This Project

Time is one of the most fundamental concepts in human existence. Throughout history, civilizations have sought to understand, measure, and master time. From ancient sundials and water clocks to mechanical marvels of the medieval age, and modern atomic clocks, humanity's relationship with time has continuously evolved.

This project explores the diverse ways humans have perceived and measured time across different eras. Dive into the past, understand the present, and imagine the future of timekeeping.
